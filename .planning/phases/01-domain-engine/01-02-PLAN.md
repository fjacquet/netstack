  <behavior>
    ## Rack Count (SIZE-02)
    - ceil(10 servers / 3 per rack) = 4 racks
    - ceil(100 / 20) = 5 racks
    - ceil(48 / 48) = 1 rack (exact division)

    ## Leaf Count (SIZE-03)
    - 2 racks = 4 leafs (always 2x racks)
    - 1 rack = 2 leafs
    - 5 racks = 10 leafs

    ## Spine Scaling (SIZE-04)
    - 2 leafs (1 rack) -> 4 spines (minimum non-blocking = uplinkPorts = 4)
    - 40 leafs (20 racks) -> 4 spines (max(4, ceil(40/32)) = max(4,2) = 4)
    - 130 leafs (65 racks) -> 5 spines (max(4, ceil(130/32)) = max(4,5) = 5)
    - Spine count is NEVER 2 for any input (prove by testing 20 racks)
    - SPINE_CAPACITY_EXCEEDED violation when leafSwitches > 32

    ## OOB Switches (SIZE-05)
    - 1 rack with 10 servers -> 1 OOB switch (10+2=12, ceil(12/48)=1)
    - 1 rack with 46 servers -> 1 OOB switch (46+2=48, exactly at limit, NO violation)
    - 1 rack with 47 servers -> 2 OOB switches (47+2=49, ceil(49/48)=2), WITH OOB_PORT_SATURATION violation
    - 3 racks with 20 servers each -> 3 OOB switches

    ## Pure Function (SIZE-06)
    - Same input called twice returns deeply equal output
    - No side effects (function has no imports from React, Zustand, or anything with state)

    ## Cable Quantities (link-based, not port-sum)
    - 1 rack: 2 leafs x 4 uplinks = 8 leaf-spine cables
    - 2 racks: 4 leafs x 4 uplinks = 16 leaf-spine cables
    - 4 racks: 8 leafs x 4 uplinks = 32 leaf-spine cables
    - serverLeafCables = totalServers (one cable per server)
    - serverOobCables = totalServers + leafSwitches (servers + leaf mgmt ports)

    ## Oversubscription Ratio
    - 48 servers at 25G: (48*25) / (4*100) = 1200/400 = 3.0
    - 10 servers at 25G: (10*25) / (4*100) = 250/400 = 0.625

    ## DAC Distance Advisory
    - DAC cable type with racks > 8 -> DAC_DISTANCE_ADVISORY violation
    - DAC cable type with racks <= 8 -> no advisory
    - AOC cable type with racks > 8 -> no advisory (only DAC triggers it)
  </behavior>
